Understanding Moment of Force

The moment of force (or torque) is a measure of the tendency of a force to rotate an object about an axis. It is calculated as:

Moment = Force × Distance

Where:

  • Force is measured in Newtons (N)
  • Distance is measured in meters (m)
  • Moment is measured in Newton-meters (N⋅m)
Note: The direction of rotation (clockwise or anticlockwise) is determined by the position of the force relative to the pivot point.
